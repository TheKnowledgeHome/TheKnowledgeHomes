This is The Knowledge Home

Operating since 2002, The Knowledge Home provides quality early childhood education through its three year old and four year old group kindergarten programs. Our primary focus is the wellbeing of every child. We provide our children the attention they need to grow and develop into happy, healthy people and be confident for school.
